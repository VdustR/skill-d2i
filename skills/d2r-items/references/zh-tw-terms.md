# Traditional Chinese Term Reference (繁中術語對照)

Community slang and official translations for D2R items. Use this when the user describes items in Chinese.

Sources: [D2R World](https://d2r.world/zh-TW), [巴哈 暗黑破壞神板](https://forum.gamer.com.tw/B.php?bsn=742)

## Misleading Terms

Terms where the obvious translation is wrong:

| Chinese | Correct item | NOT this |
|---------|-------------|----------|
| 破免(護符) | Sunder Charm (破除免疫) | Annihilus, -% enemy resistance |
| 安尼 | Annihilus (滅絕) | Andariel |
